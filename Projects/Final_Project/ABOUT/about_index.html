WHAT IS LARP GAME ?

LARP stands for the live-action role-playing game. Script Kill, also known as "Murder Mystery", is a real-scene social reasoning game that originated in Europe and America. Each player in the game has his own script, and the player needs to substitute himself into the role of the script, constantly collect clues, communicate with other players, reason carefully, and finally find the truth. The script has various types, including the script of emotional, ratiocination, horror, joy, faction. Thus the site is categorized by the number of players, and types of scripts. The users will be able to search and get to know the type of script they are interested in.
